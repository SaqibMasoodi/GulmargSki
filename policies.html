Booking & Payments

To secure your spot on any Wolf Adventure expedition, a deposit is required. We operate on a first-come, first-served basis due to limited capacity on the mountain and in our partner lodges.

  • 1

    Confirmation Deposit

    A 50% advance payment is required to confirm your booking. Until this deposit is received, your dates are not guaranteed.

  • 2

    Final Balance

    The remaining 50% must be paid at least 15 days prior to your arrival in Gulmarg. Failure to pay the balance may result in cancellation.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans change. However, due to the logistical complexity of high-altitude expeditions and hotel pre-bookings, our refund policy is strict.

Timeframe Refund Eligibility
> 30 Days before arrival 90% Refund (10% processing fee)
15 - 30 Days before arrival 50% Refund
< 15 Days before arrival No Refund

* All refunds are processed within 7-10 business days to the original payment method.

Risk & Liability Waiver

Extreme Sport Warning

Skiing, snowboarding, and high-altitude trekking are classified as extreme sports. They carry inherent risks including, but not limited to, physical injury, avalanche, hypothermia, and equipment failure.

By booking a package with Wolf Adventure, you acknowledge that you are voluntarily participating in these activities with knowledge of the dangers involved.

Wolf Adventure and its affiliates are not liable for:

  • Personal injury or death resulting from skiing/snowboarding accidents.
  • Loss or damage of personal equipment (cameras, phones, ski gear).
  • Delays or cancellations caused by "Acts of God" (heavy snowfall blocking roads, avalanche control, political unrest).

We strongly recommend all guests purchase comprehensive Travel Insurance that specifically covers winter sports and emergency helicopter evacuation.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This section outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data in compliance with standard GDPR regulations.

Data Collection

We collect only the information necessary to process your booking and ensure your safety. This includes Name, Contact Details, Emergency Contacts, and Medical Conditions relevant to high-altitude exertion.

Data Usage

We do not sell your data to third parties. Your information is shared strictly with:

  • Hotel partners (for check-in verification).
  • Transport providers (for pickup coordination).
  • Medical teams (in case of emergency).

Media Rights

Photos or videos taken by our guides during the trip may be used for Wolf Adventure marketing materials. If you prefer not to be photographed, please inform your lead guide on Day 1.
