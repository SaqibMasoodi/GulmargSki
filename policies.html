Cancellation & Refunds

Hotel-Policy Linked | Complaint-Safe

1. Hotel Policy Covers Refunds

All cancellations and refunds are governed strictly by the respective hotel’s cancellation and refund policy. Wolf Adventure does not override, modify, or guarantee hotel refund decisions.

Role of Wolf Adventure: We act solely as a facilitator/booking agent between the client and hotel. Any refundable amount approved by the hotel (if applicable) will be passed to the client after deduction of applicable service, transaction, and administrative charges.

2. Non-Refundable Components

Company service charges, instructor booking charges, equipment blocking charges, and logistics costs are non-refundable, irrespective of hotel refunds. Only the portion explicitly refunded by the hotel is eligible for return.

3. Timeline for Refunds

Refunds (if approved by hotel) will be processed within 7–15 working days after the amount is received from the hotel/vendor. Delays caused by hotels, banks, or payment gateways are beyond company control.

4. Non-Refundable Situations

No refund shall be applicable in the following cases:

  • No-show by client
  • Early departure
  • Missed training sessions
  • Cancellation due to weather, road blocks, gondola closure, or law & order situation
  • Medical issues or personal emergencies
  • Government advisories or administrative restrictions

5. Date Modification

Date change requests are subject to Hotel availability, Revised pricing, and Written confirmation. Date change approval is not a right, but a discretionary decision.

No Cash / No Guarantee Clause

Wolf Adventure does not guarantee refunds in cash or immediate refunds. Refund eligibility depends entirely on third-party vendor approvals.

Client agrees not to initiate:

  • Police complaints
  • FIRs
  • Chargebacks
  • Defamation campaigns

for refund delays or vendor denials that are clearly disclosed in these terms.

Legal & Liability

Misuse Prevention | Law-and-Order Disclaimer

Legal Awareness & Misuse Prevention

Any attempt by a client to:

  • Threaten police action without valid grounds
  • File false complaints or exaggerated allegations
  • Pressure staff through intimidation or misuse of law

will be treated as harassment and misuse of legal process. Wolf Adventure reserves the right to seek legal remedy, claim damages, and report malicious complaints to authorities.

Police, FIR & Law-and-Order Disclaimer

Kashmir is a sensitive region subject to sudden administrative decisions. The client fully understands that Police, Army, Tourism Dept., or District Administration may restrict movement or activities. Such restrictions do not constitute service failure.

No FIR, consumer complaint, or legal claim shall be valid against Wolf Adventure for actions taken under lawful government orders.

Consumer Dispute Limitation

The client agrees that dissatisfaction due to Weather, Personal expectations, Natural conditions, or Third-party hotel services does not amount to deficiency of service under consumer law.

Claims shall be limited strictly to the actual amount paid to Wolf Adventure, excluding consequential damages, mental harassment claims, or punitive demands.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This section outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data in compliance with standard GDPR regulations.

Data Collection

We collect only the information necessary to process your booking and ensure your safety. This includes Name, Contact Details, Emergency Contacts, and Medical Conditions relevant to high-altitude exertion.

Data Usage

We do not sell your data to third parties. Your information is shared strictly with:

  • Hotel partners (for check-in verification).
  • Transport providers (for pickup coordination).
  • Medical teams (in case of emergency).

Media Rights

Photos or videos taken by our instructors during the trip may be used for Wolf Adventure marketing materials. If you prefer not to be photographed, please inform your lead guide on Day 1.
