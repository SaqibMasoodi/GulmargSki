GETTING HERE

Fly to Srinagar (SXR)

The nearest airport is Srinagar International Airport. Major airlines (Indigo, Air India, Vistara) operate daily flights from Delhi (1.5 hrs).

Tip

Book a window seat on the left side (Delhi to Srinagar) for the best mountain views.

The Drive Up

Gulmarg is a 55km drive from the airport. Airport Pickup Available (Add-on). We can organize 4x4 SUVs with tire chains for you upon request.

  • Distance 55 KM
  • Time ~2 Hours
  • Taxi Cost ~₹3,000
