ATTRACTIONS & RIDES

Beyond the slopes. Explore the valley.

The World's Second Highest Cable Car

The highlight of any trip. Rise from the pine forests to the naked peaks in minutes.

Phase 1

8,500 ft

Gulmarg to Kongdoori

Takes you to a bowl-shaped valley where most beginner skiing happens. Features restaurants and snow parks.

Phase 2

14,000 ft

Kongdoori to Apharwat Peak

The main event. Breathtaking views of Nanga Parbat (9th highest mountain).
*Strictly for sightseeing or expert skiers. Weather dependent.

Adrenaline Without Skis

Snowmobiles

High-speed motorized sleds. Zoom across vast frozen snowfields with a local operator. Fast, loud, and incredibly fun.

Speed Power

ATV (Quad Bikes)

4x4 beasts with tire chains. Popular route: Tangmarg to Drung Waterfall or around Strawberry Valley.

Off-road Scenic

Iconic Spots

The "Must-Visit" locations for every traveler.

Drung Waterfall

Famous in Jan/Feb. The water freezes solid creating a massive wall of icicles.

St. Mary’s Church

100+ year old Victorian church. Looks like a fairytale when covered in snow.

Maharani Temple

Red-roofed Shiva temple on a hillock. Visible from almost everywhere in the valley.

Complete Your Itinerary

DL

Srinagar (Dal Lake)

2 hours away. The classic experience is a Shikara ride on the frozen lake and a Houseboat stay.

PH

Pahalgam

"Valley of Shepherds." Famous for scenic rivers and pine forests. A softer vibe compared to the high-altitude desert.

Pro Tip: Booking Essentials

If you book a package, ask if Gondola tickets are included. Tickets (especially Phase 2) sell out weeks in advance online. Getting them on the spot is nearly impossible.

GETTING HERE

Fly to Srinagar (SXR)

The nearest airport is Srinagar International Airport. Major airlines (Indigo, Air India, Vistara) operate daily flights from Delhi (1.5 hrs).

Tip

Book a window seat on the left side (Delhi to Srinagar) for the best mountain views.

The Drive Up

Gulmarg is a 55km drive from the airport. Airport Pickup Available (Add-on). We can organize 4x4 SUVs with tire chains for you upon request.

  • Distance 55 KM
  • Time ~2 Hours
  • Taxi Cost ~₹3,000
